WHO WE ARE?



Lunghisa Trading is a trusted logistics and transport provider providing professional freight transport and removal services across Durban, South Africa. Established in 2011, we operate a fleet consisting of two truck and trailers and a Hyundai H100 bakkie specialising in reliable freight transport, break-bulk handling, and removals.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction and affordable pricing has made us a preferred choice for individuals and businesses alike. Our mission is to deliver goods safely, efficiently and on time, while maintaining high service quality.



WHAT WE DO?



FREIGHT TRANSPORT

Reliable freight transport solutions across Durban. Our Fleet is equipped to handle commercial cargo deliveries safely and efficiently.

BREAK-BULK

Our break-bulk service is ideal for cargo that cannot be containerized. We ensure careful handling and secure transportation of loose or irregular goods.

REMOVALS

Our Hyundai H100 bakkie offers removals of up to one tonne. This is perfect for small household moves and office relocations.
